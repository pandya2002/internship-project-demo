About Us

Top Gear Bangladesh

Top Gear Bangladesh was founded in 2015 out of a need to support the growing motorcycle community. 2019 was the year that changed the bike riding scene in Bangladesh, ushering in conversations about proper riding and protective gear.

Top Gear Bangladesh was formed to serve Bangladesh riders. We provide all kinds of motorcycle protective gear including helmets, gloves, jackets, and more.

At Top Gear Bangladesh, we only offer the best items that meet global standards.

We do not sell any helmets without ECE, DOT, or SHARP and SNELL ratings.

We aim to share extensive wisdom from top riders and safety experts.
